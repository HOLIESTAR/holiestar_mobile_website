5.Keep your network available at all times

Message Peeping Tom can only catch the messages in your notification bar. So if your device receives more than one message when you turn on the network, and your messaging apps (Messenger, Whatsapp...) only show the latest one in the notification bar, then MPT will only catch the latest one. So please ensure to keep your network available at all times to receive messages perfectly.

Plus, if your device is on low power mode, network is likely to be turned off by the system. Please make sure that MPT and messaging apps are included on the whitelist.

6. Low Power Mode Settings

In order to keep your network available to receive messages normally, please make sure that MPT and messaging apps are included in the whitelist, which can avoid MPT and other messaging apps being cleared by the system when your device is on low power mode.

7. Why can‘t MPT receive messages after using for a few days?

In some android devices, apps background services will be cleared automatically by the system to enhance performance. Therefore, MPT and other messaging apps are likely to be cleared sometimes. To allow MPT to function normally, please restart your device from time to time.

8. What kind of app is likely to interfere with the performance of MPT?

Apps related to battery saving, anti-virus, performance enhancement or memory cleaner could clear or stop MPT from working in the background, so please make sure that MPT and other messaging apps are included to the whitelist.

9. Why do my group messages are categorized into personal messages?

MPT can only catch messages from notification bar. You might have noticed that sometimes messages you received only show a person’s name instead of a group name. That is the reason why MPT is unable to categorize messages into the corresponding group. We apologize for the inconvenience and thank you for your understanding.

10. Why can‘t MPT support image, video and voice messages?

Due to the regulations of security of Android system, MPT can not catch image, video and voice messages from other messaging apps. We apologize for the inconvenience and thank you for your understanding.

11. Why can‘t MPT work anymore when I’m using LINE/Messenger on PC or Mac?

LINE

If you are using LINE on PC/MAC, it will turn off the notification on your mobile device automatically so that MPT will not receive messages. In order to function normally, please follow the steps to change settings in LINE on your PC/Mac:

Go to「Setting」→「Notifications」→「Notification Rules」→ Disable「Turn off smartphone notifications when logged in to the PC version」

Facebook Messenger

If you are using Messenger website version, Facebook will turn off the notification on your mobile device automatically.

12. Why the messages I received are incomplete?

MPT can only catch the messages in your notification bar. So if the messages in the notification bar was cut due to length limits, MPT can only catch the messages it shows. We apologize for your inconvenience and thank you for your understanding.
